About Prime Tower

Prime Tower is a luxurious 9-storey residential building located in the heart of Savar's prestigious Saidia Jamia Abashon Prokolpo. Designed for modern family living, it offers premium apartments with optimal space, comfort, and security. With 4 units per floor, each home ensures privacy while providing a vibrant community lifestyle.
